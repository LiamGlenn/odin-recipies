Chicken Spaghetti

Description

This is a quick and easy chicken spaghetti recipe! You will learn how to cook the perfect cheesy, and falvorful chicken spaghetti.

Ingridients

  • 12oz pack of angel hair pasta
  • 2 cups chopped chicken breast
  • 10.75oz can condensed cream of chicken soup
  • 10oz can diced tomatoes with green chile peppers
  • 8oz processed cubed cheese
  • 4oz jar sliced mushrooms drained
  • salt and pepper

Directions

  1. Bring large pot of slightly salted water to a boil
  2. Cook angel hair pasta in the boiling water until tender yet firm to bite, approx 4-5 min
  3. Add chopped chicken, condensed soup, diced tomatoes and chile peppers, processed cheese, mushrooms, salt, and pepper
  4. Cook and stir over low heat until cheese is melted and mixture is heated through approx, 5 minutes
